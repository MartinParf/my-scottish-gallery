Our Scottish Story

Welcome to our gallery. We are Martin, Martina and our feline companion Olivia. We came to Scotland for adventure, rugged landscapes and work in the high‑tech industry.

This map is a collection of our memories. From rainy weekends on the Isle of Skye, to wandering through historic Edinburgh, all the way to everyday moments with a pint of good beer. Scotland has taught us that even in the worst weather you can discover incredible beauty.

What comes next? Even if our footsteps soon lead us back to the Beskydy mountains, the Highlands will stay in our hearts forever.
